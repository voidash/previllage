## Safe Video Claim

```text
On a Raspberry Pi 5, Gemma E2B Q4 runs locally at roughly 6-8 generated
tokens/sec. The same Pi can also run our Nepali TTS worker with warm responses
around 1.1-1.4s and our FastConformer ASR worker with warm transcription around
2.9-3.0s for a short Nepali WAV.
```

Do not claim the Pi is running the whole national RAG, WhatsApp, ASR, TTS, and
Gemma stack concurrently. The correct framing is:

```text
Pi = low-cost local edge lane and proof that offices do not need an L40 GPU.
Office computer = full kiosk, WhatsApp, ASR/TTS, RAG, and admin loop.
```
